Tropple

After a lecture on modeling the classes for a deck of cards, I spent the good part of the following weekend creating the rules for my own card game. Being the boardgame geek that I am, it brought me much joy to do this.

Modeling the classes for the game (deck, player, pile etc.) did a great deal for my current understanding of Object Oriented Programming and I was at a great advantage when it came to design the Vending Machine CLI.

I designed the cards and board in the Unity editor and created an Event Manager to listen for Actions (cards being played, turn ending). When the turn ends, the board rotates so the next player can act.
